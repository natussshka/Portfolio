THINGS I'M BUILDING

Projects

My life has always been a mix of experiments, ideas and projects. Some start as small thoughts and grow into something real. Others are simply things I love doing and sharing with others.

Here are a few projects and spaces I'm currently creating or contributing to.

Accountability Bestie

A simple but powerful idea: sometimes what we really need is someone in our corner while we build something important to us.

Through 1:1 accountability sessions I support people who have ideas, projects or life changes they want to move forward with, but struggle with structure, momentum or consistency.

Together we create clarity, realistic steps and accountability so things actually move from idea to reality.

Workshops & Group Spaces

I create small group workshops focused on personal development, confidence and self-connection.

These spaces combine reflection, conversation and practical tools that help people reconnect with themselves and explore their next steps.

Some workshops also include movement elements connected to dance and body awareness.

Dance & Creative Projects

Dance has always been an important part of my life. It's not just movement, it's confidence, expression and community.

Through dance camps, collaborations and creative events I help create spaces where people can reconnect with their bodies, energy and creativity.

Writing & Stories

Writing has always been a way for me to process experiences and share ideas.

Through blog posts and storytelling I explore topics like personal growth, life experiments, travel and the lessons that come from starting over.

These projects are constantly evolving, just like life itself.
