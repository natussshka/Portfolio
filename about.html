A little more about me

About Natalia

My life has never followed a straight line - and honestly, I like it that way.

Hi, I'm Natalia. Over the years I've lived in different countries, changed careers, worked in completely different environments and restarted my life more than once. Somewhere along the way I realized that I'm actually quite good at navigating change - and helping others do the same.

Life Experiments

I see life as a series of experiments. Some work, some don't - but all of them teach something.

  • Living and working in several countries
  • Building new careers and starting from scratch
  • Rediscovering dance and creative expression
  • Creating workshops and community spaces

These experiences shaped how I approach personal growth today: with curiosity, structure and a bit of courage.

What I'm Exploring Now

Today I focus on personal development, accountability support and creative projects.

Through 1:1 sessions and workshops I help people create clarity, structure and momentum around their ideas and goals.

I believe that many people already know what they want - they just need the right support to move forward.

Outside of work you'll usually find me dancing dancehall, traveling, drinking coffee somewhere sunny, or writing about the small and big experiments that shape life.
